G4int TG4Medium::fIfield
private

G3 tracking medium parameter 'ifield'

  • 0 no magnetic field
  • -1 user decision in guswim
  • 1 tracking performed with Runge Kutta
  • 2 tracking performed with helix
  • 3 constant magnetic field along z
